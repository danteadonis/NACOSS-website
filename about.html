About NACOSS

The Nigerian Association Of Computer Science Students (NACOSS) is a student professional body with presence in all federal and private universities across the country. The association is an umbrella for young vibrant proffessionals to showcase their skills in the technology department.
It is also an avenue to empower computer science students across the country, and to equip them with the necessary skills needed for them to make their stand in the labour market through th use of technological tools.
